GET TO KNOW US...


What is Wander Welly?

Wander Welly is the only trail-finding website you'll ever need. Simply scroll through our trail selection, use the map to find the nearest trails or pick from one of our top suggested trails - then go! It's as easy as that. We provide the most hassle-free experience when it comes to finding a trail, because more time spent searching means less time experiencing.

Our Values

The team here at Wander Welly are passionate about getting Wellingtonians out and about to experience the nature our city has to offer. Whether thats a short walk on the trails of Mount Victoria or a 2 day hike in the Tararuas - we have it all.
