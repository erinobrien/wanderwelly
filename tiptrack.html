Tip Track

This route is quickly reached by car from downtown Wellington and combines the well-known Tip Track, Red Rocks Track - Pari-Whero, and a beach section (Sinclair Head Track). The route is, in parts, seriously steep and follows exposed ridgelines with exceptional panoramic views towards the ocean as well as the town on the other side

Trail Tips

Between kilometer 6 and 10, the track is very exposed and the notorious Wellington winds are not to be underestimated. There are some rocky sections requiring some degree of surefooted-ness. The beach section is mostly gravel with a few sandy stretches
